About Me

Hey, I’m Adittya Saha, but most people know me as Adi. I’m a BCA student at Jaypee University Anoopshahr and a passionate software developer who loves turning ideas into real, working projects. I enjoy building clean, modern websites and exploring everything about frontend, backend, and full-stack development.
